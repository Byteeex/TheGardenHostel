About The Garden Hostel

Experience a cozy and welcoming environment in the heart of Tangier. Our hostel combines comfort, lush green gardens, and cultural immersion for travelers from around the world. Meet new friends, relax, and explore the city’s charm from our central location.

Discover Tangier

Tangier’s historic Medina offers vibrant streets, colorful souks, and centuries of Moroccan tradition. Staying with us allows you to experience this unique city like a local, walking its alleys, tasting its flavors, and discovering its hidden gems.
